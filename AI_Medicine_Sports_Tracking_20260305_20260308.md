### 2. 片段组学液体活检可实现乳腺癌的早期检测、分子分型及淋巴结状态评估。

- **英语标题**: Fragmentomic liquid biopsy enables early breast cancer detection, molecular subtyping and lymph node assessment.
- **期刊**: Nature communications (2026)
- **作者**: Yuxuan Zhu, Siwei Zheng, Yinkuan Shao
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41792156/

**中文摘要**: 乳腺癌仍是全球女性面临的首要健康威胁之一，而目前的筛查手段仍受限于影像学检查的可及性不足，以及在致密型乳腺中敏感性降低等问题。本研究开展了一项多中心病例–对照研究，共纳入503例乳腺癌患者和289例良性病变对照者，据此构建了TuFEst——一种基于全基因组游离DNA（cfDNA）片段组学特征的机器学习模型。TuFEst在早期乳腺癌检测中展现出优异的敏感性（95%）与特异性（78.3%），并能稳健识别恶性病变。

**英文摘要**: Breast cancer remains a leading global health concern in women, while screening is still limited by imaging accessibility and reduced sensitivity in dense breasts. Here we conduct a multicenter case-control study including 503 breast cancer patients and 289 benign controls to develop TuFEst, a machine learning model based on genome-wide cell-free DNA fragmentomic features. TuFEst achieves high sensitivity (95%) and specificity (78.3%) for early cancer detection and reliably identifies malignancies missed by conventional imaging. Extension of this framework enables non-invasive molecular subtyping (TuFEst-MS) and lymph node status prediction (TuFEst-LN), with strong performance in independent validation cohorts and imaging-pathology discordant cases. Transcriptomic profiling of paired bulk tumor samples (n&#x2009;=&#x2009;79) demonstrates that elevated TuFEst-derived cancer scores reflect tumor aggressiveness and immune-related biological programs. Together, these findings support cfDNA fragmentomics as an integrated liquid biopsy strategy for breast cancer management, enabling concurrent detection, molecular subtyping, and lymph node evaluation with potential clinical utility.

### 3. AMR-GNN：一种支持多表征学习的图神经网络框架，用于基因组层面的抗菌药物耐药性预测

- **英语标题**: AMR-GNN: a multi-representation graph neural network framework to enable genomic antimicrobial resistance prediction.
- **期刊**: Nature communications (2026)
- **作者**: Hoai-An Nguyen, Anton Y Peleg, Jessica A Wisniewski
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41792137/

**中文摘要**: 全基因组测序（Whole-genome sequencing, WGS）数据是解析抗菌药物耐药性（antimicrobial resistance, AMR）机制的宝贵资源。然而，WGS数据具有高维特性，且缺乏标准化的基因组表征方法，这已成为实现AMR表型精准预测的关键瓶颈。为充分挖掘此类高分辨率数据所蕴含的信息，我们提出了AMR-GNN——一种基于图深度学习的计算框架。该框架通过图神经网络（Graph Neural Networks, GNN）有机整合多种基因组表征形式，从而实现从基因组序列出发的AMR表型预测。

**英文摘要**: Whole-genome sequencing (WGS) data are an invaluable resource for understanding antimicrobial resistance (AMR) mechanisms. However, WGS data are high-dimensional and the lack of standardized genomic representations is a key barrier to AMR phenotype prediction. To fully explore these high-resolution data, we propose AMR-GNN, a graph deep learning-based framework that integrates multiple genomic representations with graph neural networks (GNN) to enable AMR phenotype prediction from genomic sequence data. We test AMR-GNN with Pseudomonas aeruginosa, a clinically relevant Gram-negative bacterial pathogen known for its complex AMR mechanisms. We present AMR-GNN as a proof-of-concept framework designed to address several key problems in AMR phenotype prediction with data-driven machine learning (ML) approaches, including using multiple genomic representations to enhance performance, to mitigate the influence of clonal relationships and to identify informative biomarkers to provide explainability. Follow-up validation on the largest publicly available dataset spanning both Gram-negative and Gram-positive pathogens highlights AMR-GNN's broad applicability in detecting AMR in diverse and clinically relevant pathogen-drug combinations.

### 4. 酸驱动蛋白质去溶剂化的直接证据

- **英语标题**: Direct evidence of acid-driven protein desolvation.
- **期刊**: Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America (2026)
- **作者**: Farzad Hamdi, Ioannis Skalidis, Inken Kaja Schwerin
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41785322/

**中文摘要**: 水及其调控生物分子质子化状态的能力，主导着生命体的物理化学过程，并决定其代谢功能。然而，自1974年Kuntz与Kauzmann提出pH驱动的蛋白质脱溶剂化假说以来，氨基酸残基的质子化状态如何影响蛋白质的水合结构与溶解性，迄今仍是一个尚未解决的关键科学问题。本文中，我们利用一系列不同pH值（pH 9.0至3.5）下获得的高分辨率冷冻电子显微镜（cryo-EM）结构，对某一蛋白质复合物进行了系统研究，并在这些结构中观测并分析了数千个可分辨的水分子。

**英文摘要**: Water and its ability to modulate the protonation states of biomolecules govern the physical chemistry of life, dictating their metabolic functions. However, how amino acid protonation alters protein hydration and solubility is an open question since Kuntz and Kauzmann proposed p H -driven protein desolvation in 1974. Here, in a series of high-resolution cryoelectron microscopy structures of a protein complex at different p H values (from p H 9.0 to 3.5), we examined thousands of observable hydration sites. Cryoelectron microscopy data, in agreement with constant-p H molecular dynamics simulations, show that nearly half of protein-bound waters exchanged with the bulk solvent upon acidification, with ~100 waters lost per p H unit per molecule. The loss of waters was most significant around the side chains of glutamate and aspartate residues while specific polar residues, mostly asparagine, anchored persistent waters. A positionally conserved hydration layer was observed across all p H conditions, accounting for 40% of resolved waters. Those waters displayed denser packing than less persistent waters, forming a p H -independent solvation shell. Acid-induced water exchange also displaced bound iron, providing a mechanistic link between solvation and metal release. Our findings demonstrate the core principles of acid-driven protein desolvation, resolving a 50-y-old biochemical hypothesis.

### 5. 可重构模块化机器人中的敏捷足式运动

- **英语标题**: Agile legged locomotion in reconfigurable modular robots.
- **期刊**: Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America (2026)
- **作者**: Chen Yu, David Matthews, Jingxian Wang
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41790937/

**中文摘要**: 足式机器人正日益展现出更高的敏捷性与适应性，但迄今为止，其形态多样性仍远逊于自然界中的足式动物——后者历经演化，已通过结构重排与形态重塑，成功占据数以百万计的生态位。与生物足式系统截然不同，人工足式机器人在过去十年间却呈现出显著的构型趋同现象：主流设计高度集中于经典四足与双足架构，而此类固定构型难以根据新任务需求快速重构，亦无法在遭遇损伤后自主恢复功能。本文提出“自主模块化腿部”（autonomous modular legs）：该设计兼具敏捷性与极简性，单个模块即为一个功能完备、可独立运行的腿部单元……

**英文摘要**: Legged machines are becoming increasingly agile and adaptive, but they have so far lacked the morphological diversity of legged animals, which have been rearranged and reshaped to fill millions of niches. Unlike their biological counterparts, legged machines have largely converged over the past decade to canonical quadrupedal and bipedal architectures that cannot be easily reconfigured to meet new tasks or recover from injury. Here, we introduce autonomous modular legs: agile yet minimal, single-degree-of-freedom jointed links that can learn complex dynamic behaviors and may be freely attached to form multilegged machines at the meter scale. This enables rapid repair, redesign, and recombination of highly dynamic modular agents that move quickly and acrobatically (nonquasistatically) through unstructured environments. Because each module is itself a complete agent, the bodies that contain them can sustain deep structural damage that would completely disable other legged robots. We also show how to encode the vast space of possible body configurations into a compact latent design space that can be efficiently explored, revealing a wide diversity of novel legged forms.

### 6. DiNovo：基于镜像蛋白酶与深度学习实现高覆盖度、高置信度的从头肽段测序

- **英语标题**: DiNovo enables high-coverage and high-confidence de novo peptide sequencing via mirror proteases and deep learning.
- **期刊**: Nature communications (2026)
- **作者**: Zixuan Cao, Xueli Peng, Di Zhang
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41786727/

**中文摘要**: 尽管深度学习技术的近期进展显著推动了从头肽段测序（de novo peptide sequencing）的发展，但当前基于单一蛋白酶的蛋白质组学实验仍受限于肽段碎裂不完全及蛋白质消化不充分等问题。本文提出一种名为DiNovo的软件系统，通过利用“镜像蛋白酶”（mirror proteases）之间的互补性，实现高覆盖度、高置信度的从头肽段测序。DiNovo集成了多项创新性算法，包括镜像质谱识别算法（mirror-spectra recognition algorithm）……

**英文摘要**: Despite the recent advancements driven by deep learning, de novo peptide sequencing is still constrained by incomplete peptide fragmentation and insufficient protein digestion in current single protease-based proteomic experiments. Here, we present a software system, named DiNovo, for high-coverage and high-confidence de novo peptide sequencing by leveraging the complementarity of mirror proteases. DiNovo is empowered by several innovative algorithms, including a mirror-spectra recognition algorithm independent of pre-sequencing, two sequencing algorithms based on deep learning and graph theory, respectively, and target-decoy mapping, a method for sequencing result evaluation free of prior peptide identification. Compared with the trypsin protease used alone, DiNovo using two pairs of mirror proteases leads to two to three times high-confidence amino acids sequenced. Compared with previous single-protease de novo sequencing algorithms, DiNovo achieves much higher sequence coverage. DiNovo also shows great potential as a practical and powerful alternative to database search for peptide identification with quality control.

### 7. 抑制15-羟基前列腺素脱氢酶促进软骨再生。

- **英语标题**: Inhibition of 15-hydroxy prostaglandin dehydrogenase promotes cartilage regeneration.
- **期刊**: Science (New York, N.Y.) (2026)
- **作者**: Mamta Singla, Yu Xin Wang, Elena Monti
- **类别**: 医疗应用
- **原文链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41308124/

**中文摘要**: 衰老或关节损伤可导致软骨退变及骨关节炎（osteoarthritis, OA），而目前针对该病的有效治疗手段十分有限。我们发现，在衰老或损伤小鼠的关节软骨中，15-羟基前列腺素脱氢酶（15-hydroxy prostaglandin dehydrogenase, 15-PGDH）的表达显著升高。通过小分子抑制剂（PGDHi）进行全身性或局部性抑制15-PGDH，均可促进关节软骨再生，并显著缓解OA相关疼痛。本研究结合单细胞RNA测序（single-cell RNA-sequencing）与多重免疫荧光染色（multiplexed immunofluorescence）等技术，系统解析了软骨细胞亚群的转录调控特征及其在15-PGDH抑制介导的修复过程中的动态变化。

**英文摘要**: Aging or injury to the joints can lead to cartilage degeneration and osteoarthritis (OA), for which there are limited effective treatments. We found that expression of 15-hydroxy prostaglandin dehydrogenase (15-PGDH) is increased in the articular cartilage of aged or injured mice. Both systemic and local inhibition of 15-PGDH with a small-molecule inhibitor (PGDHi) led to regeneration of articular cartilage and reduction in OA-associated pain. Using single-cell RNA-sequencing and multiplexed immunofluorescence imaging of cartilage, we identified the major chondrocyte subpopulations. Inhibition of 15-PGDH decreased hypertrophic-like chondrocytes expressing 15-PGDH and increased extracellular matrix-synthesizing articular chondrocytes. Cartilage regeneration appears to occur through gene expression changes in preexisting chondrocytes, rather than stem or progenitor cell proliferation. 15-PGDH inhibition could be a potential disease-modifying and regenerative approach for osteoarthritis.
